</TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>Office Depot, Inc. Announces Fourth Quarter and Full Year 2016 Results </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><I>Q4 2016 GAAP Diluted EPS from Continuing Operations of $0.10 versus $0.06 in Q4 2015 </I></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><I>Realizes $700 Million in OfficeMax Integration Synergies </I></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><I>Completes Sale of European Business </I></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Boca Raton, Fla., March&nbsp;1, 2017 &#151;</B>&nbsp;Office Depot, Inc. (&#147;Office Depot,&#148; or the &#147;company&#148;) (NASDAQ: ODP),&nbsp;a
leading global provider of&nbsp;office products, services, and solutions, today announced results for the fourth quarter and full year ended December&nbsp;31, 2016. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;I am very excited to assume the role of CEO and to inherit a business with such positive earnings trends. Office Depot delivered another year of
improved profitability in 2016, exceeding the full-year adjusted operating income guidance, despite experiencing substantial business disruption related to the Staples acquisition attempt,&#148; said Gerry Smith, newly appointed chief executive
officer of Office Depot. &#147;The company made significant progress against its 2016 critical priorities and achieved substantial integration synergies, thanks to the hard work, commitment and dedication of the management team and associates. I
believe we can continue this momentum in 2017, as we focus on stabilizing the top line, implementing our cost saving programs and executing on the key initiatives of the three-year strategic plan.&#148; </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Consolidated Results </B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><U>Reported (GAAP) Results </U></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Total reported sales for the fourth quarter of 2016 were $2.7 billion compared to $2.8 billion in the fourth quarter of 2015, a decrease of 2%. Sales for the
full year 2016 were $11.0 billion, a decline of 6% compared to the prior year. Fourth quarter and full year sales benefited from the impact of a 53<SUP STYLE="font-size:85%; vertical-align:top">rd</SUP> week in fiscal 2016 of approximately $143
million. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In the fourth quarter of 2016, Office Depot reported operating income of $57 million and net income of $80 million, or $0.15 per diluted share.
Net income from continuing operations was $55 million, or $0.10 per diluted share. The fourth quarter of 2016 was favorably impacted by approximately $15 million of operating income from the additional
53<SUP STYLE="font-size:85%; vertical-align:top">rd</SUP> week, primarily in the North American Retail Division. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In the fourth quarter of 2015, the
company reported operating income of $42 million and net income of $15 million, or $0.03 per diluted share. Net income from continuing operations was $31 million, or $0.06 per diluted share. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">For the full year 2016, Office Depot reported operating income of $531 million compared to operating income of $183 million in the prior year period, and net

costs incurred through 2017. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The company continues to expect to achieve approximately $500 million in adjusted operating income in fiscal 2017, a
