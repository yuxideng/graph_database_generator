<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On May&nbsp;11, 2017, the board of directors (the &#147;Board&#148;) of Apache Corporation (the &#147;Company&#148;) appointed Mr.&nbsp;Rene&nbsp;R. Joyce as a
new director of the Company&#146;s Board. Mr.&nbsp;Joyce has been appointed to the Management Development and Compensation Committee of the Company&#146;s Board. On May&nbsp;11, 2017, the Company issued a press release announcing this appointment, a
copy of which is filed herewith as Exhibit 99.1. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">For his service, Mr.&nbsp;Joyce will receive compensation that is commensurate with that received by the
Company&#146;s other non-employee directors. Such compensatory arrangements are described under the caption &#147;Director Compensation&#148; in the Company&#146;s definitive proxy statement relating to its 2017 annual meeting of stockholders, which
was filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on March&nbsp;28, 2017, and is incorporated by reference herein. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Other than as disclosed herein, no
material plan, contract, or arrangement was entered into or materially amended, and there was no grant or award to Mr.&nbsp;Joyce or modification thereto under any such plan, contract, or arrangement, in connection with Mr.&nbsp;Joyce&#146;s
appointment. Mr.&nbsp;Joyce has (i)&nbsp;no arrangements or understandings with any other person pursuant to which he was selected as a director and (ii)&nbsp;no direct or indirect material interest in any transaction or series of similar
transactions contemplated by Item&nbsp;404(a) of Regulation S-K. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Mr.&nbsp;Joyce currently serves as a director of the midstream company Targa Resources
Corporation (&#147;TRC&#148;). Mr.&nbsp;Joyce previously served as executive chairman of the board of TRC between 2012 and 2014 and as chief executive officer of TRC between 2005 and 2011. In addition to his leadership roles with TRC and several of
its subsidiaries, Mr.&nbsp;Joyce previously served as president of onshore pipeline operations of Coral Energy, LLC, a subsidiary of Shell Oil Company (&#147;Shell&#148;) and as president of energy services of Coral Energy Holding, L.P.
(&#147;Coral&#148;), a subsidiary of Shell which was the gas and power marketing joint venture between Shell and Tejas Gas Corporation (&#147;Tejas&#148;), a natural gas pipeline company. Prior to Shell&#146;s acquisition of Tejas in 1998,
Mr.&nbsp;Joyce served as president of various operating subsidiaries of Tejas. Mr.&nbsp;Joyce holds a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering from Louisiana State University and a J.D. from Loyola University School of Law. </P>

</TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>APACHE APPOINTS RENE R. JOYCE TO BOARD OF DIRECTORS </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">HOUSTON, May&nbsp;11, 2017 &#150; Apache Corporation (NYSE, Nasdaq: APA) today announced the appointment of Rene R. Joyce to its board of
directors. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Joyce, 69, currently serves as a director of midstream company Targa Resources Corporation (TRC). Joyce previously served as
executive chairman of the board of TRC between 2012 and 2014 and as chief executive officer of TRC between 2005 and 2011. In addition to his leadership roles with TRC and several of its subsidiaries, Joyce previously served as president of onshore
pipeline operations of Coral Energy, LLC, a subsidiary of Shell Oil Company; and as president of energy services of Coral Energy Holding, LP, also a subsidiary of Shell and the gas and power marketing joint venture between Shell and Tejas Gas
Corporation, a natural gas pipeline company. Prior to Shell&#146;s acquisition of Tejas in 1998, Joyce served as president of various operating subsidiaries of Tejas. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Joyce holds a Bachelor of Science degree in mechanical engineering from Louisiana State University and a law degree from Loyola University.
